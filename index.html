Meet James Gemayel

Welcome to my portfolio. I’m James, an aspiring architect who believes that every building should tell a story. With a strong foundation in Economics and a current focus on earning my degree in Architecture, I strive to blend creativity with practicality in every project I undertake.

I'm not just focused on academics—I’ve actively immersed myself in the world of design and construction. My journey has taken me beyond the classroom, allowing me to refine my skills in furniture design and 3D rendering through hands-on experience.

When I’m not sketching blueprints or rendering virtual spaces, I take on a leadership role as the Vice President of the Byblos Campus Student Council. Architecture, it turns out, pairs well with a strong sense of initiative and community engagement.

Academic Journey

I began my academic journey with a Bachelor of Science in Economics, building a strong foundation in financial and analytical skills. I later transitioned into Architecture to pursue my passion for design and urban planning, combining both disciplines to approach projects with a balanced and informed perspective.

Alongside my studies, I am minoring in Construction Management, further enhancing my expertise in project execution, planning, and development.

I also completed an internship at Algeco Ltd, where I gained hands-on experience in aluminum fabrication and installation, deepening my understanding of material applications and construction processes.

Beyond academics, I have been an active leader in student governance, serving as the Student Council Representative for the School of Architecture and Design, the Student Council Representative for the Jbeil Library, and the Vice President of the Jbeil Student Council. These roles reflect my strong commitment to advocacy, student engagement, and community development.
