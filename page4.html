Academic Journey

James Gemayel began his academic journey with a Bachelor of Science in Economics, gaining a strong foundation in financial and analytical skills before transitioning into Architecture to pursue his passion for design and urban planning.

Alongside his studies, he is minoring in Construction Management, further strengthening his expertise in project execution and development. 

He also completed an internship at Algeco Ltd, where he gained hands-on experience in aluminum fabrication and installation.

Beyond academics, James has been an active leader in student governance, serving as the Student Council Representative for the School of Architecture and Design, the Student Council Representative for the Jbeil Library, and the Vice President of the Jbeil Student Council, demonstrating his commitment to advocacy and student engagement.
